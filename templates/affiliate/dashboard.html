SkyCareLink Partnership Terms & Conditions

1. Partnership Agreement:
By accepting these terms, you agree to become an authorized affiliate partner of SkyCareLink, providing air medical transport services under our network.

2. Revenue Sharing:
Affiliates receive compensation based on successful bookings facilitated through the SkyCareLink platform. Commission rates vary by service type and booking volume.

3. Service Standards:
All affiliate partners must maintain proper medical certifications, aircraft safety standards, and provide timely responses to quote requests.

4. Quote Management:
Affiliates are expected to provide accurate quotes within 2 hours of receiving requests and honor quoted prices for the specified validity period.

5. Patient Privacy & HIPAA:
All partners must comply with HIPAA regulations and maintain strict confidentiality of patient health information (PHI).

6. Communication Requirements:
Partners must maintain accessible communication channels during business hours and respond to booking confirmations promptly.

7. Termination:
Either party may terminate this partnership with 30 days written notice. All pending bookings must be honored through completion.
