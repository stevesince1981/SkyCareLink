Notes

The left panel uses min-h-[260px] md:min-h-[360px] to keep a nearly square feel on desktop and a compact tile on mobile.

The right panel is your advisory message, converted into scannable bullets with a clear header and a “Learn more” link.

Fully accessible: buttons/links have aria-labels; icons are marked decorative via aria-hidden.
