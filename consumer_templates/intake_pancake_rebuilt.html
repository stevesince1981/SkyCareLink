Terms of Service
Important: By using SkyCareLink services, you acknowledge that we are an information aggregation platform connecting patients with medical transport providers.
1. Service Description

SkyCareLink operates as an information aggregation and connection platform for medical air transport services. We do not own, operate, or control any aircraft or medical transport equipment. All transport services are provided by independent affiliate providers.

2. Limitation of Liability

SkyCareLink disclaims all liability for:

  • Medical outcomes, patient safety, or quality of care during transport
  • Aircraft safety, mechanical issues, or operational delays
  • Pricing adjustments, additional fees, or billing disputes with affiliates
  • Cancellations, delays, or changes to transport schedules
  • Any wrongdoing, negligence, or misconduct by affiliate providers
3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

Patient Death or Medical Contraindication: In the unfortunate event of patient death before transport, refunds are handled directly by the affiliate provider.

Cancellation Timeframes:

  • 24+ hours before transport: Full or partial refunds may be available
  • Less than 24 hours: Most affiliates impose cancellation fees
  • Day of transport: Significant cancellation penalties typically apply

All refund requests must be submitted directly to the affiliate provider. SkyCareLink does not process refunds.

Privacy Policy
HIPAA Notice: SkyCareLink operates as a business associate and maintains strict PHI protection standards.
1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, contact information, medical information necessary for transport coordination, payment and billing information, and location data for pick-up and delivery coordination.

2. How We Use Your Information
  • Transport Coordination: Connecting you with appropriate medical transport providers
  • Communication: Updates on transport status, quotes, and service notifications
  • Platform Improvement: Analytics to enhance user experience and service quality
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and safety standards
3. Information Sharing

With Affiliate Providers: We share necessary medical and contact information with selected transport providers to facilitate your transport request.

We Do NOT Share: Information with unauthorized third parties, marketing lists, medical information for non-transport purposes, or personal data for commercial gain.

4. Data Security

SkyCareLink implements industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption for all PHI transmission, multi-factor authentication, regular security audits, secure data storage, and HIPAA-compliant data handling procedures.
