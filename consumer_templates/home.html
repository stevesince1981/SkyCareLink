Know your rights before you choose an air ambulance

  • Providers should give a written price quote before a flight (except true emergencies).
  • Quotes should clearly list services and all charges you will incur.
  • Avoid providers who refuse to provide a written quote.
  • Comparing quotes helps you review costs, certifications, and qualifications.
  • Ask questions. This is an important medical decision—make it an informed one.
