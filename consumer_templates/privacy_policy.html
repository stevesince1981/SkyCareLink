Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 11, 2025

HIPAA Notice: MediFly operates as a business associate and maintains strict PHI protection standards.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, contact information, and emergency contacts
  • Medical information necessary for transport coordination
  • Payment and billing information
  • Location data for pick-up and delivery coordination

Technical Information:

  • IP addresses, browser type, and device information
  • Platform usage analytics and interaction data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Transport Coordination: Connecting you with appropriate medical transport providers
  • Communication: Updates on transport status, quotes, and service notifications
  • Platform Improvement: Analytics to enhance user experience and service quality
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and safety standards
  • Customer Support: Resolving inquiries and providing assistance

3. Information Sharing

With Affiliate Providers: We share necessary medical and contact information with selected transport providers to facilitate your transport request.

We Do NOT Share:

  • Information with unauthorized third parties
  • Marketing lists or contact databases
  • Medical information for non-transport purposes
  • Personal data for commercial gain

4. Data Security

MediFly implements industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all PHI transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure data storage with limited access controls
  • HIPAA-compliant data handling procedures

5. Data Retention

  • Active Transport Records: Retained for 7 years as required by medical records laws
  • Account Information: Retained until account deletion is requested
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for platform improvement
  • Communication Logs: Retained for 3 years for quality assurance

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Platform functionality and user preferences
  • Analytics to improve service delivery
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with:

  • Payment Processors: For secure transaction processing
  • Mapping Services: For location and routing information
  • Communication Tools: For notifications and updates
  • Analytics Platforms: For service improvement (anonymized data only)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@medifly.com
  • Phone: 1-800-MEDIFLY
  • Mail: MediFly Privacy Officer, [Company Address]

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notification 30 days before implementation.
