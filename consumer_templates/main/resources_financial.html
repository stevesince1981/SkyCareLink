Financing Options for Medical Transport

Air medical transport costs can be significant. These resources may help you explore financing options for your medical transport needs.

Additional Considerations
  • Insurance Coverage: Check with your insurance provider first - many policies cover emergency medical transport
  • Payment Plans: Many medical transport providers offer direct payment plan options
  • Medical Savings Accounts: HSA or FSA funds can often be used for medical transport expenses
  • Employer Benefits: Some employers offer emergency assistance programs or flexible spending accounts
  • Non-Profit Organizations: Certain charities provide assistance for medical transportation costs
